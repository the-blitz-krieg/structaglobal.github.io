Quick Answers

Frequently Asked

What types of architectural materials does Structa Global supply?
+

Structa Global supplies a comprehensive portfolio including high-performance facade systems, precision-engineered structural components, bespoke interior finishes, sustainable building materials, roofing and waterproofing solutions, and premium architectural hardware — all certified to international standards including ISO, CE, BIS, and relevant Indian codes.

Where does Structa Global operate?
+

We are headquartered in Kochi with active offices and service teams in Trivandrum, Thrissur, Kozhikode, and Kottayam. Our supply network extends across South India and serves projects nationally and internationally.

Does Structa Global work with architects and interior designers directly?
+

Absolutely. We consider ourselves an extension of your design team. Our technical consultants engage from the specification stage — helping architects and interior designers select the right materials early, avoiding costly changes during construction.

Do you supply sustainable and green-certified building materials?
+

Yes. Sustainability is core to our ethos. We stock and source products certified for IGBC, LEED, and GRIHA green building rating systems, including low-carbon concrete alternatives, sustainably sourced timber, and energy-efficient facade systems.

How do I get a product specification or quotation?
+

Simply use the contact form below, call our Kochi office, or email our team. We typically respond within one business day with the relevant product data sheets, certifications, lead time estimates, and pricing.
