How We Verify Quality

Our 4-Stage
Quality Assurance

Every product that enters our portfolio has cleared a rigorous four-stage qualification process - before a single unit is delivered to any project in Kochi, Trivandrum, Thrissur, Kozhikode or Kottayam.

Request Product Certifications →
01
Manufacturer Qualification

Before any product enters our portfolio, we audit the manufacturer - reviewing their quality systems, production processes, certification status, and international supply track record.

02
Independent Testing

All materials undergo independent third-party laboratory testing before specification. We verify performance data against claimed certifications - never taking a manufacturer's word alone.

03
Pre-Shipment Inspection

Every major order is subject to pre-shipment inspection - verifying product conformance, quantity accuracy, packaging integrity, and documentation completeness before goods leave the factory.

04
Delivery & Documentation

Every delivery is accompanied by a full certification package - ensuring complete traceability and compliance documentation for your project records across all five Kerala locations.

  • Material Test Reports (MTR) from accredited laboratories
  • Mill certificates with heat / batch traceability
  • CE Declaration of Performance (DoP)
  • BIS / IS code compliance certificates
  • IGBC / LEED product contribution data sheets
  • Material Safety Data Sheets (MSDS)
  • Manufacturer warranty documentation
Our Guarantee

If It's in Our Portfolio, It's Already Proven.

We never supply materials that haven't cleared our full qualification process. If you receive it from Structa Global - whether in Kochi, Trivandrum, Thrissur, Kozhikode or Kottayam - it has been tested, certified, inspected, and documented.
